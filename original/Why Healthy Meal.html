Why Healthy Meals?

From The Heart

A nutritious, well-balanced diet – along with physical
activity and refraining from smoking – is the foundation
of good health. Healthy eating includes
consuminghigh-quality proteins, carbohydrates,
heart-healthy fats,vitamins, minerals and water in the
foods you take in while minimizing processed foods,
saturated fats and alcohol. Eating in this manner
helps you maintain your body’s everyday functions,
promotes optimal body weight and can assist in disease prevention.
